SolutionOS Agentic Operating Layer

A simple view of how SOS helps a human and an AI agent keep project memory, files, decisions, and outputs in a known shape.

Human

Asks questions, drops material, approves gates, and chooses what matters.

SOS Assistant

Explains the next safe choice and routes the user to the right workflow.

SOS Commands

Use /sos:summary, /sos:audit, /sos:archive, and other workflows.

Project Context

vault/triage

Raw, messy, or not-yet-classified material waiting for a human-gated decision.

vault/wiki

Curated, durable knowledge that should help future humans and agents resume work.

vault/archive

Non-active retained material, source evidence, email trails, old notes, and historic records.

vault/outbox

Generated deliverables, exports, handoffs, reports, and context packages.
